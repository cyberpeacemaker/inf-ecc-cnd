    - notifiying on threshold levels of the risk
    - backward looking view on risk events
# 2.Risk Management Program
- Risk Identification: internal and extrenal risk
- Risk Assessment: likelihood and impact, quantitative and qualitative
- Risk Matrix: probability, likelihood, consequence/impact
- Risk Treatment: based on severity level, results of risk assessment | [eliminate:zero, mitigate:reducing]
- Risk Tracking & Review: handle new risk, evaluate implemented strategies
